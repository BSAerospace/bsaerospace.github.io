Interested in Sponsorship?

BSA is focused on fostering community for black and allied students, providing professional opportunities for our membership, and diversifying the STEM workforce through outreach for students interested in engineering, aerospace-related industries, and aviation in general. In the past academic year, we had over 50 members across over 12 majors.

We are looking to expand our network of collaborators and would love to work more with different companies! There are various avenues of collaboration that we can explore. We would be happy to discuss collaboration ideas for events and programming in the upcoming academic year. We offer corporate sponsorship for companies that would like closer interactions with Black Students in Aerospace. Becoming a corporate sponsor comes with a number of incentives and benefits, many of which involve programming with our membership. More details are outlined in our attached Sponsorship Packet below for the 2024-2025 school year.

If you would like to discuss collaboration or are interested in being a sponsor, please reach out to bsaerospace@umich.edu or pass it forward to others at your company. Thank you for your time and looking forward to hearing back!
