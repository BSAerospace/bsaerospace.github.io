Our Founders

Co-Founder

Erin Levesque

She/They

Erin graduated cum laude in April 2022 with a BSE in Aerospace Engineering. Erin created BSA in February 2020, and she worked tirelessly to build community among Black Aerospace students at UMich.

Co-Founder

Prince Kuevor

He/Him

Prince completed his bachelor's and master's degrees in Aerospace Engineering along with his PhD at the University of Michigan, focusing on flight dynamics and controls. He served BSA very well as a co-founder, and as our first Aerospace Department Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) Committee Liaison.
